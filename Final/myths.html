Myths vs Reality

Myth

Black cats bring bad luck.

Reality

In many cultures, black cats are actually symbols of good fortune.

Myth

They are connected to witchcraft.

Reality

This belief came from medieval fear and superstition, not fact.

Support Black Cat Adoption

Because of long-standing superstitions, black cats are often overlooked in animal shelters. This means they may wait longer to be adopted compared to other cats.

In reality, black cats are just as affectionate, playful, and unique as any other cat. Choosing to adopt one not only gives them a loving home, but also helps challenge outdated myths.

If you're considering adopting a pet, take a closer look at black cats, you might find the perfect companion :)
