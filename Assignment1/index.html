The Hobbit: An Unexpected Journey

A film directed by Peter Jackson

Inspired by John Ronald Reuel Tolkien Novel (s)

Act 1

The start of the story begins with meeting on of our main characters, Bilbo. We also get a glimpse into his home, The Shire. Gandalf makes an unexpected visit to Bilbo as well as the 13 dwarves that will make up the Thorin Oakenshield Comapny. Lastly, Bilbo is offered the proposition of an adventure. Which after some begrudgement he accepts and we are launched into Act.

Act 3

After Bilbo rejoins the Comapny they are quickly after being attacked by Orcs. They are chased up the trees at the edge of the cliff. Thorin comes close to death and Bilbo shows his bravery by trying to protect Thorin. After Gandalfs call for help they are saved by a group of eagles.
